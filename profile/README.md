## What We Build

AI search is replacing traditional search. When users ask ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity for recommendations, your brand either gets cited or it doesn't. There is no page two — there is only mentioned or invisible.

**Voyage builds open-source infrastructure that helps companies monitor, optimize, and monetize how AI models represent their brand.** We call this space **GEO** (Generative Engine Optimization) — and we're building the protocol layer to make it programmable.

## How It Works

```
Research & Analyze  →  Site Audit  →  Content Generation  →  Automated Delivery
```

1. **Research & Analyze** — Deep analysis of how major LLMs perceive your brand vs. competitors across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity.
2. **Site Audit** — Examine your content through an AI-crawler lens to surface visibility gaps and structural issues.
3. **Content Generation** — Generate and dynamically update optimized pages targeting specific LLM query patterns.
4. **Automated Delivery** — AI agents open pull requests directly to your repositories — no manual integration required.

## The GEOFi Network

Traditional SEO operates on clicks and impressions. AI search has no equivalent attribution layer — until now.

**GEOFi** (Generative Engine Optimization Finance) is our protocol for real-time attribution and micro-payments when AI models consume and cite content. Built on top of Coinbase's [x402](https://github.com/coinbase/x402) payments protocol, it creates an economic layer where content creators are compensated for AI-driven discovery.
